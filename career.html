Job Offer

Cleaner

We are looking for a diligent and dedicated Cleaner to maintain cleanliness and hygiene standards at The Artisan Bakehouse. As a Cleaner, you will be responsible for ensuring the bakery premises are clean, presentable, and welcoming to our customers.


Responsibilities:

  • Perform routine cleaning tasks, such as sweeping, mopping, and dusting.
  • Ensure restrooms and dining areas are clean and sanitised.
  • Keep the kitchen area and equipment tidy.
  • Assist in maintaining inventory of cleaning supplies.

Requirements:

  • Male/Female within the age range of 18-40 years old.
  • Attention to detail and a strong work ethic.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team.
  • Physical stamina to handle various cleaning tasks.

Account Clerk

We are seeking an organised and detail-oriented Account Clerk to support our financial records and ensuring compliance with accounting standards.


Responsibilities:

  • Process accounts payable and accounts receivable transactions.
  • Assist in preparing financial reports and statements.
  • Maintain accurate records of financial transactions.
  • Assist in budget preparation and monitoring.

Requirements:

  • Male/Female within the age range of 18-35 years old.
  • SPM/STPM/Accounting diploma/degree or any qualifications in a related field.
  • Previous experience in bookkeeping or accounting preferred.
  • Proficiency in accounting software and MS Excel.
  • Attention to detail and strong analytical skills.
